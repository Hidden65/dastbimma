About Dastbi MMA Gym

DASTBI MMA, founded in 2017, is an institute of Martial Arts & fitness that provides quality training to build a stronger generation and encourage people of all ages and abilities to take up MMA and other combat to develop individually and master the art of self-defense. Currently we offer trainings for MMA (Mixed Martial Arts), Muay Thai which is recognized by the Olympics, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (Gi & non-Gi), Kyokushin Karate (world's strongest Karate), CQC (Self-protection system), Animal Flow to improve strength, power, flexibility, mobility and coordination. Callisthenic, Kettlebell, Free Hand Exercises, Strength and conditioning Training and many more. We also organize seminars, boot camps, free self-defense training sessions for women under the 'Divas to Defense Program.
